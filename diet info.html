Diet Information

Explore various types of diets and learn how each one can help you achieve your health and fitness goals.

Types of Diets

Keto Diet

A low-carb, high-fat diet that aims to trigger ketosis in the body to burn fat efficiently.

Mediterranean Diet

Focuses on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats like olive oil. Great for heart health.

Intermittent Fasting

A time-based eating pattern that alternates between periods of fasting and eating.

High-Protein Diet

Emphasizes eating more protein-rich foods like meat, dairy, eggs, and legumes to build muscle and stay full.
