RECIPES

EGGS

Heat pan with a little fat. Crack egg in. Cook gently until white is set, yolk is runny. Slide onto plate. Enjoy!

  • 1 egg
  • 10 g butter
  • 1 pinch salt

EGYPTIAN FAVE BEANS

Ful medames is an Egyptian dish of slowly cooked fava beans. Soaked, then cooked until tender and seasoned. Served hot with vegetables and pita bread.

  • 100 g fava beans
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 0.5 lemon

FLUFFY PANCAKE

Mix dry ingredients. Add milk, egg, and butter. Stir until combined. Cook on pan until golden brown. Serve with toppings!

  • 60 g flour
  • 1 egg
  • 75 ml milk
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp butter
Recipe video
LIVER SANDWICHES

Sauté sliced liver with seasonings (and peppers/garlic for Alexandrian style). Put cooked liver in bread. Add toppings. Enjoy!

  • 100 g liver
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 bun
  • 0.5 bell pepper
Recipe video
CHEESE PIES

"Feteer Bel Gebna" is Egyptian cheese pie made with layered dough and cheeses. Baked till golden. Enjoy as meal/snack.

  • 100 g dough
  • 50 g cheese
  • 1 tsp butter
Recipe video
PASTRIES

Pastries are made by combining flour, fat, liquid, and sometimes sugar/leavening. Bake until golden. Enjoy!

  • 100 g flour
  • 50 g butter
  • 30 ml water
  • 1 pinch salt
Recipe video
CROISSANTS

Croissants are buttery, crescent-shaped flaky pastries. Bake until golden and crisp. Enjoy!

  • 100 g flour
  • 75 g cold butter
  • 30 ml milk
  • 1 tsp yeast
Recipe video
FETEER MESHALTET

Egyptian layered pastry made from stretched dough brushed with ghee. Bake till golden. Enjoy!

  • 150 g flour
  • 50 g ghee
  • 60 ml water
Recipe video
FRUIT SALAD

Chop fruits. Mix in bowl. Add citrus juice (optional). Chill and enjoy!

  • 100 g apple
  • 100 g banana
  • 50 g grapes
  • 10 ml lemon juice

AVOCADO TOAST

Toast bread. Mash avocado with salt/pepper. Spread and enjoy!

  • 1 toast slice
  • 0.5 avocado
