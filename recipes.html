RECIPES

EGGS

Heat pan with a little fat. Crack egg in. Cook gently until white is set, yolk is runny. Slide onto plate. Enjoy!

EGYPTIAN FAVE BEANS

Ful medames is an Egyptian dish of slowly cooked fava beans. They are soaked, then cooked until tender, often mashed, and seasoned with olive oil, lemon, garlic, and spices. Served hot with fresh vegetables and pita bread, it's a popular and hearty meal. Enjoy!

FLUFFY PANCAKE

Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until just combined (don't overmix). Pour small amounts of batter onto a hot, lightly greased pan. Cook until bubbles appear and the edges are set, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown. Serve with your favorite toppings. Enjoy!

LIVER SANDWICHES

Sauté sliced liver with seasonings (and peppers/garlic for Alexandrian style). Put cooked liver in bread. Add desired toppings. Enjoy!

CHEESE PIES

"Feteer Bel Gebna" is Egyptian cheese pie made with layered dough and various cheeses, baked until golden and melty. Enjoy as a meal or snack. Enjoy!

PASTRIES

Pastries are made by combining flour, fat, liquid, and sometimes sugar/leavening. Flaky types need layering fat and dough. Richer doughs with fillings are also common. Bake until golden. Enjoy!

Recipe video
CROISSANTS

Croissants are buttery, crescent-shaped flaky pastries. Layering cold butter into dough creates their airy texture. Bake until golden and crisp. Enjoy!

Recipe video
FETEER MESHALTET

Feteer Meshaltet is Egyptian layered pastry made from stretched thin dough brushed with ghee. Multiple folds create many flaky layers. Bake until puffed and golden. Serve hot, plain or with toppings. Enjoy

FRUIT SALAD

Chop your favorite fruits. Mix them in a bowl. Add a squeeze of citrus (optional). Chill and serve. Enjoy!
